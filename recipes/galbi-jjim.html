Galbi Jjim

Description

A juicy, tender and very flavorful beef short rib recipe. This dish is traditionally served on special occasions as it takes a while to cook in order for the beef to become very tender. A shortcut to cut down on the time is to use a pressure cooker.

Ingredients

  • beef short ribs
  • carrots, chopped
  • daikon, cubed
  • dried jujube
  • green onion, chopped
  • shiitake mushrooms
  • soy sauce
  • mirin
  • sugar
  • garlic, minced
  • ginger

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and briefly blanch the short ribs
  2. Drain the water and add soy sauce, ginger, garlic, mirin, sugar, shiitake mushrooms and jujube
  3. Cover and cook on medium heat for 30 minutes
  4. Add the carrots and daikon, lower the heat to a simmer, cover and continue to cook for 1 hour
  5. Check intermittently to make sure the sauce has not dried out completely during this process. Add a little water if necessary
  6. Remove the lid and cook on medium heat to evaporate most of the liquid, leaving some sauce remaining
  7. Sprinkle green onions on top and serve with rice
