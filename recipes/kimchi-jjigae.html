Kimchi Jjigae (Kimchi Stew)

Description

A hearty Korean staple that will warm you up from the inside out. This dish is a quintessential Korean stew that can be served as both a side dish and a main meal.

Ingredients

  • kimchi
  • kimchi brine
  • pork belly
  • tofu
  • green onions
  • yellow onion
  • gochugaru (Korean hot pepper flakes)
  • sesame oil
  • soup soy sauce
