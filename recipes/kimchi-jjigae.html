Kimchi Jjigae (Kimchi Stew)

Description

A hearty Korean staple that will warm you up from the inside out. This dish is a quintessential Korean stew that can be served as both a side dish and a main meal.

Ingredients

  • kimchi
  • kimchi brine
  • sliced pork belly
  • tofu
  • green onions
  • yellow onion
  • carrots
  • garlic
  • gochugaru (Korean hot pepper flakes)
  • sesame oil
  • soup soy sauce

Steps

  1. Add sesame oil and garlic into the pot
  2. Saut√© sliced pork belly until lightly browned outside
  3. Add the kimchi to the pot and cook until it turns slightly yellow
  4. Add the yellow onions, carrots, gochugaru, kimchi brine, and soup soy sauce
  5. Pour in enough water to cover all of the ingredients and let simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Add the tofu and simmer for another 10 minutes
  7. Serve and top with green onions
