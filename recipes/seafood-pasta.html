Seafood Pasta

Description

A simple weeknight seafood pasta recipe.

Ingredients

  • whole wheat pasta of your choice
  • tomato sauce
  • shrimp
  • mussels
  • bell pepper
  • carrots
  • yellow onion
  • garlic
  • black pepper
  • red chili flakes
  • olive oil
  • mozarella, shredded

Steps

  1. Chop the bell pepper, carrots, onion, and garlic
  2. Place a pot of water on the stove over medium-high heat
  3. Place a pan on medium heat and throw in the chopped vegetables with a bit of olive oil
  4. Once the water is boiling, toss in the pasta and cook for the recommended duration of time (typically around 8 minutes)
  5. With about 2 minutes remaining for the pasta, add the shrimp and mussels to the pan. Slightly char each side of the shrimp and wait for the mussels to open up
  6. Pour the tomato sauce into the pan and let simmer
  7. Strain the pasta and plate onto individual dishes
  8. Top the pasta with the sauce and sprinkle mozarella over
